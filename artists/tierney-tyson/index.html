bio

Tierney describes herself as a “conceptual entrepreneur,” which turns out to be a catch-all for a culturally engaged practice that cuts across mediums (video, installation, language), platforms (lectures, museums, publishing, the internet), and into deeply entrenched issues of identity, social injustice, and digital culture. For the New Museum Triennial earlier this year, Tyson presented JQuery, an installation that surfaced narratives of race in America by mining pop culture. This September, Bridget Donahue will host her first New York solo show, “Regex Elevated".
