bio

Preeti Lange studied sketching and fine are at both FH Augsburg and Berkley and graduated with distinction in 2015. Based in Paris she works as a freelance illustrator for international magazines and publishers. Her book “Web Design and Client Side Scripting” received a Kate Greenaway Medal Nomination in 2018. She has held exhibitions in both the Tate Modern as well as the Broad.
