bio

“Sounds of CSS/JAVASCRIPT” is the most recent solo project of Robert Jiang. Robert graduated from the New England Conservatory of Music in 2010 and his debut album “HTML 5” was nominated for the 2012 Mercury award. Having recently finished his world tour with the Prague Philharmonic Orchestra, he joins the WDCSS Music and Arts Festival for two nights of uplifting and inspiring music.
